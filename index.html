02 About Me

I capture moments that tell extraordinary stories

I'm Himanshu Singh Bais, a passionate content storyteller and photographer based in India. With a keen eye for light, composition, and emotion, I transform ordinary moments into extraordinary visual narratives.

My work spans photography, content creation, visual storytelling, and video editing. I believe every frame tells a story — and I'm here to make that story unforgettable. From capturing the golden hour to crafting compelling digital content, I bring creative vision to every project.

Beyond the lens, I'm also an author, having written pieces on finding extraordinary hope and the will to persevere. Every creative pursuit feeds into my philosophy: Light. Composition. Click.
